# Privacy Policy Case

**Last Updated:** April 08, 2026

DKG Labs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Fitness & Smart Ring application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Health and Fitness Data
The app collects health data primarily via your paired Smart Ring and Android Health Connect, including:
- Steps and physical activity
- Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
- Sleep duration and sleep stages
- Blood oxygen levels (SpO2)
- Body temperature

### 1.2 Profile Information
When you create an account, we may collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Date of birth
- Gender
- Height and weight (to calculate fitness metrics)

### 1.3 Location Data
We require location permissions to:
1. Scan for and connect to your Bluetooth Smart Ring.
2. Provide GPS tracking for outdoor activities (if activated by the user).

## 2. How We Use Information
We use the collected data to:
- Synchronize your Smart Ring data with the app.
- Provide personalized wellness insights via our AI Fitness Coach.
- Track your long-term health trends and streaks.
- Improve the performance and features of the application.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- **Local Storage**: Much of your health data is stored locally on your device in a secure database.
- **Cloud Storage**: We sync data with a secure backend (Firebase/Supabase) to provide backup and multi-device access.
- **Security**: We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data during transmission and storage.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Authentication**: To manage your user account.
- **Google Play Services (Health Connect)**: To share and receive health data with other wellness apps at your request.
- **Supabase**: To store synced fitness metrics and journal entries.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices
- **Access and Delete**: You can view and edit your profile data in the app's settings. You may request account deletion by contacting support.
- **Permissions**: You can revoke Bluetooth, Location, or Health Connect permissions at any time via your device settings, though this may limit app functionality.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the application.
